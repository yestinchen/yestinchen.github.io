Bio

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science at Seattle University. My research interests are in the data management, novel database systems, and applications of machine learning for database systems.
I obtained my Ph.D. from York University, Canada, in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaohui Yu. During my Ph.D. studies, I have also received valuable guidance and feedback from Prof. Nick Koudas at the University of Toronto. Prior to that, I earned my B.S. in Software Engineering in 2014 and M.S.E. in Computer Science in 2017, from Shandong University, China.
