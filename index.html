Bio

I am an assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science at Seattle University. My research interests are in the data management, novel database systems, and applications of machine learning for database systems.
I obtained my Ph.D. from York University, Canada, in 2023, under the supervision of Prof. Xiaohui Yu. Prior to that, I received my BS in Software Engineering in 2014 and MSE in Computer Science in 2017, from Shandong University, China.
